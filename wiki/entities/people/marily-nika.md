# Dr. Marily Nika

> Product coach specializing in **AI product management**. Profiled in *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* Ch 34. PhD in machine learning from **Imperial College London**; 8 years at [[google|Google]] building AI + AR/VR products; 2 years at Meta Reality Labs. Teaches AI product management through her own courses and at **Harvard Business School**.

## Role here
One of the seven product coaches Cagan personally profiles in Ch 34. The AI PM specialist in the referral network — matters especially for [[data-product-management|data / AI / platform PM work]].

## Path (per Ch 34)
- Grew up in Greece; discovered her brother's BASIC book at age 7 on the family Amstrad; knew from a young age she wanted to study computer science.
- Greek academic system enrolled her in economics instead; she persisted; mentors told her *"it matters less what you study in school and more where your passion is."*
- Won a **Google scholarship**; PhD in machine learning at **Imperial College London**.
- Joined Google in 2013 without knowing what product management was; became mesmerized by working with PMs.
- **Internal PM rotation program** at Google let her transition from a technical role to product.
- 8 years at Google (London + Silicon Valley) building **AI products for Google Assistant** and **AR/VR**; 2 years at **Meta's Reality Labs**.

## Journey to coaching (per Ch 34)
Colleagues sought her out for career + AI-product mentoring; expanded beyond her employers; then to writing + teaching. Today: product coach, popular speaker at major conferences; teaches **AI product management** through her own online courses and at **Harvard Business School**.
