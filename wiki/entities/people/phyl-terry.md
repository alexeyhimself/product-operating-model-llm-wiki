# Phyl Terry

> Product coach specializing in **product leadership** and community. Profiled in *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* Ch 34. Founder of **Collaborative Gain** (2003) — the largest peer-network community of product leaders in the world. Author of *Customers Included* and *Never Search Alone: The Job Seeker's Playbook*.

## Role here
One of the seven product coaches Cagan personally profiles in Ch 34. Represents the *product-leadership-through-community* lane — coaching product leaders and connecting them into a peer network.

## Path (per Ch 34)
- Part of the first startup **Amazon** bought.
- Brief stint at **McKinsey**.
- CEO of **Creative Good** (CG) for **15 years** — a premier partner working with top product teams and companies to get closer to customers through hands-on user research (the company's *"listening labs"* technique).
- CG engaged with **American Express, Apple, BBC, Facebook, Google, Microsoft, Nike, and The New York Times**, among others.
- Personal engagement not only with product leaders and teams but with senior executive teams — sometimes reluctant participants.

## Journey to coaching (per Ch 34)
Post-dot-com bubble: decided *"magic could happen when product leaders connect with one another."* In **2003 founded Collaborative Gain**; 20 years later it is the largest peer-network of product leaders — several hundred product leaders from product companies worldwide.

## Views on coaching (per Ch 34)
Good coaching goes well beyond good listening: knowledge of the substance; continuous-learning mindset; willingness to roll up sleeves (coach + coachee); deep understanding of company politics and culture; willingness to have difficult and uncomfortable interactions. *"But most of all, it requires real thinking — thinking hard as you formulate your coaching advice and coaching the coachee in how to think through hard problems."*

## Publications
- *Customers Included* — user research + product decisions.
- *Never Search Alone: The Job Seeker's Playbook* — plus a free volunteer-driven global community of PMs and others growing their careers.
