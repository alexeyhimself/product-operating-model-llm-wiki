# Paweł Huryn

> Product writer/coach (The Product Compass) whose two posts served as the **supporting material** used to populate this wiki's first principles. The principles themselves belong to **[[svpg|SVPG]] / [[marty-cagan|Cagan]] via _[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]_** — Huryn is a secondary explainer, not the source.

## Role here
A **supporting explainer**, not an originator. His two ProductCompass posts summarize and comment on the first principles from *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]*, and include a useful Q&A with [[marty-cagan|Cagan]]. The wiki cites them as supporting provenance with links; it attributes the principles to TRANSFORMED/SVPG.

Two framing choices of his that the wiki notes (and does not adopt as canon):
- Packages the principles as **20 in 5 groups of 4** with compact labels — his framing of the book, not verbatim headings.
- Lists **five** product risks (adds Ethics), while quoting Cagan's case for **four** — see [[the-four-big-risks]].
