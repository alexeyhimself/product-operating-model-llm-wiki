# Anders Toxboe

> Copenhagen-based product coach; founder & editor of **[[learning-loop|Learning Loop]]** (learningloop.io). Not part of the SVPG canon, but present in this wiki as the author of a **useful, coach-oriented catalog** of discovery/validation techniques the wiki uses as a **supporting** menu alongside [[inspired|INSPIRED]] Part IV.

## Contributions & positions
- **Idea Validation Playbook** — a public catalog of **89 product experiments** organised into four validation stages (**problem · market · product · willingness-to-pay**) with scored evidence strength, difficulty, and mapping to Desirability / Feasibility / Viability. Sixty of the plays are also published as the **Validation Patterns** printed card deck. See [[discovery-techniques-catalog]] for the wiki-integrated version.
- Uses the **Desirability / Feasibility / Viability** design-thinking triangle (IDEO/Osterwalder lineage) rather than Cagan's four risks. The wiki catalog **remaps** desirability → **value or usability** per Cagan (see [[the-four-big-risks]]).
- **"Evidence strength" as a numerical score (5–95).** Cagan-compatible in spirit (his four value-test signals — money > reputation > time > access — express the same intuition), but Toxboe formalises it into a scalar. Useful pedagogy for novice PMs; see the *"evidence strength ≠ Cagan alignment"* note on [[discovery-techniques-catalog]].

## Why they're in this wiki (voice-discipline note)
Toxboe is the wiki's **first non-SVPG-canonical source**. Attributed here as a **supporting reference** — not primary canon. Techniques in his catalog are tagged with a **Cagan-alignment** label (Endorsed · Compatible · Use-with-care) so a coach can reason about the technique in the wiki's four-risk frame. When Cagan and Toxboe conflict — e.g. NPS/PMF Survey/Focus Group — the wiki flags the conflict and defers to Cagan.
