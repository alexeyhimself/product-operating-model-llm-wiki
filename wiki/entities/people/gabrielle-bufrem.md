# Gabrielle Bufrem

> Product coach specializing in **product leadership development**. Profiled in *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* Ch 34 (Transformation Help). Born and raised in Brazil, educated at Brown University; speaks four languages fluently; has lived and worked in ten countries and shipped products across nine industries.

## Role here
One of the seven product coaches Cagan personally profiles in Ch 34 as examples of the "type of person to look for" — specifically a **product leadership coach** by Cagan's typology. Represents SVPG's coaching-referral network.

## Path (per Ch 34)
- Fell in love with product during a Google internship (originally in marketing).
- First PM role at Education First (Boston + Switzerland).
- Five years at **Pivotal Labs** (Silicon Valley, Paris, Singapore) as PM then product leader.
- **First head of product and design** at **Little Otter Health** (mental-health mission-based startup).
- Realized her superpower was coaching + developing people who led product organizations — moved into product coaching full-time.

## Reputation (per Ch 34)
Kindness plus toughness. Quickly builds strong personal relationships and trust; delivers frank, honest, critical feedback that helps coachees break past old limits.
