# Petra Wille

> Product coach specializing in **PM and product-leader development**. Profiled in *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* Ch 34. Germany-based; author of *STRONG Product People: A Complete Guide to Developing Great Product Managers* (2020). Active organizer of the European product community. Also co-authored [[2025-06-10-torres-wille-what-is-product-ops|the Torres–Wille discussion on product ops]] already ingested here as supporting canon.

## Role here
One of the seven product coaches Cagan personally profiles in Ch 34. Represents the *PM and product-leader development* lane in the European product community.

## Path (per Ch 34)
- Began in engineering; transitioned to product management to work across the whole build-lifecycle *"from concept to launch."*
- Product leader at two major German tech-powered companies: **XING** and **tolingo**.
- In leadership, took on real coaching responsibilities and discovered coaching's dual benefit — people development *plus* talent attraction and retention.

## Journey to coaching (per Ch 34)
Transition into full-time coaching *"felt like a natural next step."* Workshops + coaching sessions; developed her own toolkit of coaching strategies. Coaches across healthcare tech, e-commerce, container shipping, and others. Petra's coaching philosophy (per Ch 34): *"A strong product coach meets people exactly where they stand… This ability comes from a foundation rooted in real-world experiences in product, providing a bedrock of understanding from which they can offer relevant and actionable coaching and advice."*

## Publications & community
- ***STRONG Product People*** (2020) — a complete guide to developing great product managers.
- Active leader in the **European product community** — speaks at and organizes popular product conferences.
