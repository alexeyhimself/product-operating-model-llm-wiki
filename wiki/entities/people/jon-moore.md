# Jon Moore

> SVPG partner and co-author of *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* (with Cagan, Hickman, Idiodi, and Chris Jones). The wiki's canon for the three transformation dimensions — **changing how you build / solve problems / decide which problems to solve** — comes from his three-part 2022 SVPG series.

## Role here
Primary source for the [[svpg-product-operating-model-introduction|three transformation dimensions]] decomposition. His articles articulate **why** each shift matters and what good looks like, in plain enough language for non-technical executives to follow. Cagan's [[2024-01-17-cagan-product-model-concepts|Product Model Concepts]] and [[2023-03-15-cagan-product-model-competencies|Product Model Competencies]] articles point at Moore's three pieces as the dimension-level reference.

## Articles ingested (primary)
- [[2022-09-17-moore-changing-how-you-build]] — the *Build* dimension; small/frequent/reliable releases ([link](https://www.svpg.com/changing-how-you-build/)).
- [[2022-09-20-moore-changing-how-you-solve-problems]] — the *Solve Problems* dimension; feature teams vs. empowered teams ([link](https://www.svpg.com/changing-how-you-solve-problems/)).
- [[2022-09-23-moore-changing-how-you-decide-which-problems-to-solve]] — the *Decide* dimension; vision → strategy → team objectives ([link](https://www.svpg.com/changing-how-you-decide-which-problems-to-solve/)).
