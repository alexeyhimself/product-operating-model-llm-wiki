# Jon Moore

> SVPG partner and co-author of *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* (with Cagan, Hickman, Idiodi, and Chris Jones). The wiki's canon for the three transformation dimensions — **changing how you build / solve problems / decide which problems to solve** — comes from his 2022 SVPG transformation series, **rooted in his earlier May 2022 *Transformation Defined* article** that introduced the framing.

## Role here
**Root author** of the [[2022-05-31-moore-transformation-defined|three-dimensions framing]] of transformation. The Sep 2022 deep-dives are the per-dimension expansions. Per the [[2023-02-20-cagan-transformed-announcement|TRANSFORMED announcement]], Moore had been the original second author of *TRANSFORMED* before passing the project to Cagan; his transformation series previewed the book's framing. Also led a successful pre-Internet transformation before joining SVPG (per the same announcement) — one of the first-person case studies in *TRANSFORMED*.
