# Hope Gurion

> Product coach — product leadership + continuous discovery. Profiled in *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* Ch 34. Named on-page as the coach who worked with the [[almosafer|Almosafer team]] during the [[almosafer|istiraha marketplace innovation]] (Ch 14).

## Role here
One of the seven product coaches Cagan personally profiles in Ch 34. **Two-lane coach:** product leadership coaching + continuous discovery coaching (the latter through work with Teresa Torres, author of [[continuous-discovery-habits|*Continuous Discovery Habits*]]).

## Path (per Ch 34)
- Began in product in the early internet era — online shopping, real estate, job/career services.
- Broad business background (business development, sales, marketing, advertising, finance) plus product passion.
- Built and led product organizations at **CareerBuilder** and **Beachbody** — repeatedly transforming stakeholder-driven feature teams into product-model organizations.
- **2018:** made the decision to become a full-time product leadership coach.
- Hosts the **Fearless Product Leadership** podcast; coaches continuous discovery alongside Teresa Torres.

## Themes (per Ch 34)
Business-and-product intersection: helping leaders + teams understand the dynamics of their business and precisely how their product work drives business results. Coaches for the *transformative shift in perspective* — the moment when a leader grasps the heart of a persistent challenge and can confront it head-on.
