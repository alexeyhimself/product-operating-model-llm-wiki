# Good Strategy Bad Strategy

> Richard Rumelt's classic on what real strategy is (a diagnosis + a guiding policy + coherent action) versus the fluff, goals, and slogans that pass for it — "bad strategy."

## Why it's recommended
One of only two strategy books Cagan endorses in [[2020-02-22-cagan-product-strategy-the-missing-link|"Product Strategy: The Missing Link"]] — most, he says, are "paint-by-numbers." Rumelt's core distinctions map directly onto Cagan's product-strategy pillars: **[[focus|focus]] is not prioritization**, and strategy means identifying the few critical factors and concentrating action on them.
