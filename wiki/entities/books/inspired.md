# INSPIRED

> Cagan's foundational book on **how to create products customers love** — the product-management classic (2nd ed. 2017).

## What it covers
The work of the product manager and the product team: empowered teams, the [[the-four-big-risks|four big risks]], and [[product-discovery|product discovery]] techniques. Audience: product managers and teams.

## Key contributions
- The **four big risks** (value, usability, feasibility, business viability) referenced by [[assess-product-risks]].
- The idea-hit-rate reality cited in [[minimize-waste]]: "at least half" of ideas won't work; good teams assume "three quarters" won't perform as hoped.
