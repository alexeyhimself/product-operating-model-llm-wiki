# LOVED

> *LOVED: How to Rethink Marketing for Tech Products* — [[martina-lauchengco|Martina Lauchengco]], 2022. The **product-marketing** volume of the Silicon Valley Product Group series ([[inspired]] → [[empowered]] → *LOVED* → [[transformed]]).

## What it covers
What great **[[product-marketing|product marketing]]** is and does: positioning, messaging, market fit (the *market* side of product-market fit), go-to-market strategy, and the discipline that connects a valuable product to the customers who should love it. The counterpart to *INSPIRED* (product teams) and *EMPOWERED* (leaders): the role most product managers need a partner for, especially with a direct sales channel.

## Key contributions
- Establishes **product marketing as a first-class competency**, distinct from brand, field, and performance marketing.
- Names the failure mode Cagan flags in the [[2022-03-07-cagan-product-ops-dan-olsen|Product Ops fireside]]: companies with a real need for complex product marketing but no one who wants to own it — leading to a "product-marketing rebranding" of product ops.
