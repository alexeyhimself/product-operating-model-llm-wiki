# Principles: Life and Work

> Ray Dalio's distilled work-and-life **principles** plus the radical-transparency / meritocracy culture of Bridgewater.

## Why it's recommended
**[[marty-cagan|Cagan]] called it the most important business book of 2017** and encourages the teams he coaches to read it ([[svpg-cagan-on-principles-dalio|source]]). It models the same conviction behind this wiki: write down and reason from **principles**, because techniques change but principles endure.

## Best for (topics)
Decision-making, leadership, building a principle-driven [[product-culture|culture]]; pairs with [[principles-over-process]]. **Caveat (Cagan):** adopt the *principles*, not Bridgewater's extreme radical-transparency culture, which rarely transplants to tech companies.
