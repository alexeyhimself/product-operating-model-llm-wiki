# EMPOWERED

> Cagan & Chris Jones on **product leadership** — how leaders create the conditions for empowered, ordinary teams to do extraordinary work (2020).

## What it covers
The role of leadership and management in the product model: coaching, staffing, vision and strategy, and empowering teams. Audience: product leaders and managers.

## Key contributions
- The definition this wiki leans on in [[empowered-with-problems-to-solve]]: an empowered team is "given a problem to solve, rather than solutions to build, and... held accountable for the results."
- Frames empowerment, coaching, and leading with context that *TRANSFORMED* later folds into the [[principles-of-product-culture|culture principles]].
