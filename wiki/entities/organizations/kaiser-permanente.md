# Kaiser Permanente

> One of the largest US nonprofit healthcare + coverage organizations, serving **12M+ patients**. Digital-org transformation to the product operating model began **2019**. The 2020 [[kaiser-permanente-get-care-now|Get Care Now]] case study — nationwide 24/7 on-demand virtual care launched **four months from inception** — is Cagan's proof against the "we can't do this in a regulated industry" objection ([[overcoming-objections|Ch 46]]).

## Profile
- US nonprofit healthcare + coverage; 12M+ patients across many regional markets.
- Digital-org transformation began 2019, focused initially on **reimagining the patient health journey**.
- Regional-market operational structure — the transformation had to work across many markets with different licensing and clinical practices.

## Why it's here
Exemplar (regulated-industry innovation) — and a specific counter to the [[overcoming-objections|Part X Ch 46 objection]]: *"we're in a regulated industry — we can't test with customers / we can't release this often."*
