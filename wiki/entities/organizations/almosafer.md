# Almosafer

> Saudi-Arabia-based travel and tourism company (part of **Seera Group**), serving the Middle Eastern region. One of the wiki's [[almosafer|case studies]] because it is the book's proof that the product model can produce world-class innovation far from Silicon Valley — and its 2020 pandemic-response innovation (the **istiraha marketplace**) is a *new solution to a new problem* Cagan uses to illustrate the extra difficulty of that case.

## Profile
- Travel + tourism; regional focus on Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, expanding across Middle East.
- Part of **Seera Group**.
- Started as brick-and-mortar travel agencies serving personal, business, government, and religious travel.
- Transformation to the product model began **2018**, led by product leader **Ronnie Varghese** and technology leader **Qais Amori**.
- Coached by **Hope Gurion** during the istiraha innovation (Hope is profiled in *TRANSFORMED* Ch 34).

## Why it's here
Exemplar. Cagan's frame in *[[transformed|TRANSFORMED]]* Ch 14: *"talent can be found all over the world, and when these people move to the product model, they are capable of remarkable levels of innovation."* The wiki uses Almosafer as the counter to "the model doesn't work in our region."
