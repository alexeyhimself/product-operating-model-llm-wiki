# CarMax

> Largest used-car retailer in the US, founded in 1993 (spun off from Circuit City), based in Richmond, VA. Brand built on **no-haggle pricing, 125-point inspection, 7-day money-back, 30-day warranty**. Included in the wiki because its pandemic-era pivot to end-to-end digital retail is *TRANSFORMED*'s clearest example of a company using pre-existing empowered product teams to execute a national omnichannel change in six months. See [[carmax]] for the full case.

## Profile
- Public US automotive retailer; 200+ stores as of 2020.
- Consumer-facing digital-experience transformation led by **Ann Yauger** (carmax.com team), predating the pandemic.
- CPO Tyler Tuite endorsed the book's release (front-matter endorsement).

## Why it's here
Exemplar. Companies that have already transformed can respond to crisis with speed. CarMax launched its full digital transaction flow — virtual customer experience center, instant-offer trade-in, curbside/touchless/home delivery, "love your car" 90-day warranty + 30-day returns — **nationally within six months** of pandemic onset, after April 2020 sales fell 75%+.
