# Silicon Valley Product Group (SVPG)

> The firm founded by [[marty-cagan|Marty Cagan]] whose books, articles, and partners are the **canonical source** of the Product Operating Model this wiki teaches.

## Profile
A small group of experienced product leaders who write, speak, and coach on how the best product companies work. Their published material (books + svpg.com articles) is the **"SVPG canon"** voice this wiki cites and holds the user's org against. Partners referenced across the model include Chris Jones, Christian Idiodi, Lea Hickman, Martina Lauchengco, and Jon Moore.

## Why it's here
The reference standard. When this wiki says "SVPG canon," it means positions traceable to SVPG sources (e.g. *[[transformed]]*, *[[inspired]]*, *[[empowered]]*, and svpg.com). The coach uses this canon as the bar — see [[overview]] and the [[diagnostics]] rubrics.
