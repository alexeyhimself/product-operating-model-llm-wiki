# Gympass

> Brazilian corporate-fitness benefit marketplace, founded 2012 with the mission to **"defeat inactivity."** Corporate clients pay for their employees to access a network of gyms and studios; over **50,000+ providers across 11 countries** at the time of *TRANSFORMED*. Included in the wiki because the 2020 home-wellness pivot ([[gympass-wellness-pivot|case study]]) is a canonical example of a small empowered team executing a whole-business pivot in weeks — enabled by the 2018 transformation.

## Profile
- Brazilian scale-up; global corporate-wellness marketplace.
- Transformation began 2018 under CPO **Joca Torres**, alongside CTO + CMO.
- Now known as **Wellhub** (rebrand post-book, noted for graph reference).
- Reached **US $2.2B valuation** during the pandemic; one of the LatAm tech success stories.

## Why it's here
Exemplar. Cagan uses Gympass to make two points: (1) **crisis-driven pivot in weeks** is achievable when the muscles are already there — the pivot was executed by 1 PM + 1 designer + 4 engineers + BizOps + PMM; (2) **build-and-partner** as an explicit strategic choice — Gympass built the marketplace and partner integrations, and partnered for the vertical wellness content.
