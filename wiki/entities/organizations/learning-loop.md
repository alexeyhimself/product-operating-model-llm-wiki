# Learning Loop

> Copenhagen-based product-education platform (learningloop.io) run by [[anders-toxboe|Anders Toxboe]]. Publishes free **playbooks** for product teams — including the **Idea Validation Playbook** this wiki's [[discovery-techniques-catalog]] draws from.

## Profile
- **Industry:** product-management / product-design education.
- **Founder / editor:** [[anders-toxboe|Anders Toxboe]].
- **Base:** Copenhagen, Denmark.
- **Business model:** free playbooks online + printed **card-deck products** (Validation Patterns, Discovery Patterns, etc.) sold via shop.learningloop.io.

## Why it's here
Learning Loop is a **supporting reference** — not part of SVPG canon. The wiki uses its Idea Validation Playbook as a **broader menu of discovery / validation techniques** a coach can draw from alongside [[product-discovery-techniques|Cagan's canonical catalog]] ([[inspired|INSPIRED]] Part IV).

## What the wiki uses
- **89-play Idea Validation Playbook** organised into four validation stages:
  - [Validate the problem](https://learningloop.io/playbooks/validating-the-problem)
  - [Validate the market](https://learningloop.io/playbooks/validating-the-market)
  - [Validate the product](https://learningloop.io/playbooks/validating-the-product)
  - [Validate willingness to pay](https://learningloop.io/playbooks/validating-willingness-to-pay)
- Each play page includes: How · Why · Validates (Desirability/Feasibility/Viability) · Difficulty · **Evidence Strength** (numerical 5–95) · See Also · Also Called · Relevant Metrics · real-life examples with sourced citations · related plays.
- **The 60-play Validation Patterns card deck** is a subset of the 89 plays — the subset that seeded [[2026-aleksei-validation-patterns-sheet|Aleksei's working sheet]].
