# Trainline

> UK-based rail ticketing platform, founded 1997, sold first online ticket 1999. Acquired by **KKR** in January 2015 and transformed under CEO **Clare Gilmartin** (from eBay), CTO **Mark Holt**, and CPO **[[jon-moore|Jon Moore]]**. The wiki's canonical [[trainline-transformation|end-to-end transformation case]] because Moore's first-person account walks all three dimensions of change in explicit detail.

## Profile
- Rail ticketing marketplace, initially UK-only, expanded to European geographies + coach (bus).
- **Purchased by KKR January 2015** (private equity) for <£500M; **IPO'd on London Stock Exchange ~4 years later at just over £2B** — one of the year's largest European IPOs.
- CEO Clare Gilmartin was recruited from eBay; her first CEO role.
- CPO Jon Moore led this transformation *before joining* [[svpg|SVPG]]; the story is in *TRANSFORMED* Part V (by Moore) and Ch 49 (price-prediction innovation).

## Why it's here
Exemplar. Both a **transformation exemplar** (KKR-to-IPO in ~4 years is the clearest end-to-end story in the book) and an **empowered-engineer exemplar** (the hack-day less-crowded-trains live-data prototype that led to an AWS re:Invent headline showcase introduced by Werner Vogels).
