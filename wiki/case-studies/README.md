# wiki/case-studies/ — transformations and real examples

One page per concrete example: a company transformation story, a team that moved from feature-factory to empowered, a product decision and its outcome. Cases here are **SVPG-published / real named-company examples** (Amazon, Spotify, Google, Apple, …) — this wiki is **org-agnostic**. Your own organization's examples do **not** live here; they belong in your org's own [strategic-context baseline](../concepts/strategic-context.md) / context store, supplied to the agent separately.

Use the [`case-study`](../../templates/case-study.md) template.

A good case study: context / starting state, what changed, which [`principles`](../principles/) and [`concepts`](../concepts/) it illustrates, the outcome, and the transferable lesson.
