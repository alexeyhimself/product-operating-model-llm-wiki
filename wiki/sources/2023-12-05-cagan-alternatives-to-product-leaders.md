# Cagan — Alternatives To Product Leaders

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Alternatives To Product Leaders" · svpg.com, 2023-12-05 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/alternatives-to-product-leaders/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Cagan's most explicit statement that there is **no real alternative** to strong product leaders at scale. Names the three observed substitutes — command-and-control, leading through process, leading through proxies — and argues each consistently fails. Includes the early-stage caveat: founder-as-head-of-product can work; premature outside-hire often fails.
