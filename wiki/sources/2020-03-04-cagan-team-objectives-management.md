# Cagan — Team Objectives: Management

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Team Objectives – Management" · svpg.com, 2020-03-04 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/team-objectives-management/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Objectives need **active management** once set. The core mechanism is **weekly OKR check-ins** (where are we, what's next, where do we need help) so weeks don't slip by. Leaders resolve conflicts/dependencies promptly with minimal big meetings; each person still gets ongoing **coaching**; the PM keeps their manager informed; teams **raise commitment risk early**; and teams **help colleagues** because in tech companies "we either all succeed or none of us succeed."

## Notes for the coach
- **Weekly tracking is the anti-drift mechanism** — the single most-skipped habit that makes OKRs "go nowhere."
- Manage by coaching; regressing to command-and-control here forfeits the empowerment benefit.
