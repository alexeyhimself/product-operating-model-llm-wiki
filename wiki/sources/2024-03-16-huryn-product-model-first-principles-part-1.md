## Notes for the coach
- When asked "what's the source of this principle?", lead with **TRANSFORMED ([[transformed]])** as the root and offer this post as a supporting explainer with a link.
- Cagan's Q&A (in this post) clarifies: product strategy has two facets — choosing the most important problems, and **placing bets**; **value proposition is orthogonal** to strategy; **business strategy is the input** to product strategy; insights from discovery feed the next strategy iteration.
