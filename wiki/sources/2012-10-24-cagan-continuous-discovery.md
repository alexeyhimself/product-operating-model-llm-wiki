# Cagan — Continuous Discovery

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Continuous Discovery" · svpg.com, 2012-10-24 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/continuous-discovery/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Argues for continuous discovery and continuous delivery as the operating pattern — discovery continuously generates validated backlog items; delivery continuously builds, tests, deploys. Notes that this pattern often pushes teams from Scrum toward Kanban or Scrum-with-Kanban adaptations.

## Notes for the coach
- **Process choice follows operating model.** When teams adopt continuous discovery and delivery, Scrum's time-boxes start to feel limiting; Kanban or hybrid emerges naturally.
- **Continuous deployment = gentle deployment** when paired with customer-impact assessment — change is small, easier to localize, easier to roll back.
