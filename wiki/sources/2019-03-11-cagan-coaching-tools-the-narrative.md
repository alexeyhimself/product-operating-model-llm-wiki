# Cagan — Coaching Tools: The Narrative

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Coaching Tools — The Narrative" · svpg.com, 2019-03-11 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/coaching-tools-the-narrative/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Cagan names the **written narrative** as his single favorite coaching tool — the Amazon 6-pager pattern adapted for PM coaching. Forces homework, exposes logic gaps, and replaces the "highest paid person in the room" with a real argument. The most-resisted of all coaching techniques, and the one the resisters need most.
