## Notes for the coach
- **"The only two things that get in the way of good work":** (1) people don't know *how* to do good work → the leader's job is **coaching**; (2) they know how but *aren't motivated* → the leader's job is to provide vision / strategic context. These map to the two product-leadership responsibilities (coaching + leadership).
