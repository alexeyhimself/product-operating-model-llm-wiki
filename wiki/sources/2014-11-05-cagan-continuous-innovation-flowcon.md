# Cagan — Continuous Innovation (FlowCon keynote)

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan, keynote "Continuous Innovation" at FlowCon · YouTube, published 2014-11-05 — [watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x6-cfEGFMOM). Slim provenance card; transcript **not reproduced**.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon) — an early (2014) statement of the through-line.** One of the earliest recorded talks framing the purpose of strong product teams as **continuous innovation on behalf of customers** — the theme the wiki later crystallizes as [[the-point-is-outcomes]].

## What it backs
- concepts: [[empowered-product-teams]], [[the-point-is-outcomes]] (consistent innovation as the point), [[product-discovery]] (discovery as the innovation engine), [[missionaries-vs-mercenaries]]

## Notes for the coach
- Value as **provenance for the continuity of the message** — the core model predates the "product operating model" naming by nearly a decade.
