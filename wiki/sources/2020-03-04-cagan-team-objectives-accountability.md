# Cagan — Team Objectives: Accountability

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Team Objectives – Accountability" · svpg.com, 2020-03-04 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/team-objectives-accountability/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The **companion to empowerment is accountability**. The key nuance: **accountability is proportional to ambition.** Failing a **moon shot** is largely expected; failing a **roof shot** — or worse, a **high-integrity commitment** — is where accountability bites. Substantial failures get a **"team objective post-mortem"** (treated like an outage: root cause, what should have been done differently, peers from impacted teams present).

## What it backs

## Notes for the coach
- **Empowerment without accountability is not the model** — teams own the solution *and* answer for roof-shot / committed results.
- Post-mortems are constructive, not punitive — but the mild discomfort is itself a reason to take commitments seriously.
