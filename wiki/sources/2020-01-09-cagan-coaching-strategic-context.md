# Cagan — Coaching: Strategic Context

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Coaching — Strategic Context" · svpg.com, 2020-01-09 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/coaching-strategic-context/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Names the **six elements of strategic context** every PM needs from leadership: company mission, company scorecard, company objectives, product vision, product strategy, product principles. Empowered teams cannot make good decisions without this context — providing it is a leadership job, not a PM job.

- competencies: [[product-leadership]]

## Notes for the coach
- **The six elements are a checklist.** Run a PM through them — if any are missing or unclear, the leadership has a gap to close.
- **Onboarding artifact:** strategic context is part of every new PM's onboarding.
- Useful at multi-business-unit companies (Amazon's AWS vs. e-commerce) — each BU has its own strategic context.
