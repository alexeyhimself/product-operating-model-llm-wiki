## Notes for the coach
- **Meaningful vs optimization experiments.** The Prime experiments (Super Saver Shipping → annual-fee free shipping) tackled real value/viability risk over 2+ years — *not* button-colour A/B tests. Use as the counter-example to "experimentation = optimization."
- **Single-Threaded Leaders (STLs)** — Amazon's term for durable, empowered leaders with strong [[product-sense|product sense]]; "leaders develop leaders and take seriously their role in coaching others" (Leadership Principles). Cagan: "more than anything else … the key to Amazon's ongoing success."
- **The empowered engineer** — Charlie Ward (an engineer) connected the DVD-team's subscription tech to free shipping and proposed the subscription that became Prime. Canonical [[empowered-engineers|empowered-engineer]] story.
- **PRFAQ + six-pager** — Amazon's vision + written-narrative techniques; ties to [[2019-03-11-cagan-coaching-tools-the-narrative|the narrative]] and [[working-backwards]].
- **Long-term thinking + "true agile"** — bets with multi-year payoffs; teams "extremely agile as measured by what's meaningful," rarely talk about Agile-the-process.
- Prime results: ~$35B subscription revenue, 220M subscribers, ~45% of Amazon marketplace GMV (SVPG estimates).
