# Cagan — Product Strategy: Actions

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Strategy — Actions" · svpg.com, 2020-02-20 (Pillar 3 of 4) — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-strategy-actions/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Pillar 3 of the strategy series. The fork-in-the-road moment: convert insights into **roadmaps + feature teams** or **team objectives + empowered teams**. Argues the OKR system is the formalization technique but the deeper requirement is empowered teams and a real strategy.

## Notes for the coach
- **OKRs are not magic** — they only work over empowered teams with a real strategy. Otherwise: mercenary OKRs / feature-OKRs.
- **The fork:** feature-by-name (close to obvious) vs. problem-with-outcome (closer to empowerment) — the distinction can be subtle but matters.
- **You don't strictly need OKRs** — Cagan: a knowledgeable leader sitting down with each team to share context and assign problems can work.
