## Notes for the coach
- **Origin of the term:** confirms **Atlassian** used "product operating model" first; Cagan adopted it (and "product model" for short) because a *model* is not a *process*.
- **Spotify-model myth:** tribes/squads are "funny Swedish terms"; what actually matters is that Spotify lives the product-model principles. Same for the Amazon/Apple/Netflix "religions" — strip the culture-specific rhetoric and the same principles remain.
- A clean, self-contained statement of 3 dimensions (build / solve / decide) + 4 competencies (PM, designer, tech lead, product leader) + 5 concepts + 20 principles. Excellent provenance for [[overview]].
