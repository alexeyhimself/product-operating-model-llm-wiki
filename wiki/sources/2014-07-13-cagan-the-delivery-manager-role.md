# Cagan — The Delivery Manager Role

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "The Delivery Manager Role" · svpg.com, 2014-07-13 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/the-delivery-manager-role/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Cagan re-brands the project-manager role as **delivery manager** — purpose is unambiguous (get stuff shipped + production support), distinct from discovery and from Agile-coach work. Diagnoses the three reasons project management has a bad rap (Waterfall certifications, confusion with Agile-coach role, non-technical impediment-chasers).

## Notes for the coach
- **In small startups**, PM owns project management. In larger orgs, delivery manager is the canonical answer.
- **The PM/delivery-manager partnership** is what frees the PM to do discovery ([[2019-06-28-cagan-coaching-managing-time|canon]]).
- Delivery manager is *not* an Agile coach — common confusion.
