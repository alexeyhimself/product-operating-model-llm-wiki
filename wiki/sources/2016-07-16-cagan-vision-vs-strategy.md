# Cagan — Vision vs. Strategy

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Vision vs. Strategy" · svpg.com, 2016-07-16 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/vision-vs-strategy/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The cleanest single-page distinction between **product vision** (the future we're creating; 2–5 years; persuasive, inspiring; "stubborn on vision, flexible on details") and **product strategy** (the plan for getting there). Source of the four-step strategy decomposition (choices → insights → action → management) that the 2020 four-pillar series elaborates.

## Notes for the coach
- **Quote magnet (Bezos via Cagan):** "Stubborn on the vision, flexible on the details."
- **Vision is recruiting tool, not spec.**
- The "leadership-vs-management" analogy (vision = inspiring; strategy = intentional) is a useful frame.
