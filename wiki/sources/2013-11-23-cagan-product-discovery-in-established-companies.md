# Cagan — Product Discovery in Established Companies

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Discovery in Established Companies" · svpg.com, 2013-11-23 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-discovery-in-established-companies/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The discovery toolkit applied to mature products with revenue, brand, and customer base to protect. Two duties: (1) protect revenue and brand (small-percentage A/B tests, invitation-only live-data tests, NDA customer programs); (2) protect employees and customers (gentle deployment, customer-impact assessment). Argues against "corporate innovation labs" as a workaround.

## Notes for the coach
- **Quote magnet:** "If you stop moving forward you will die."
- **Argument against innovation labs:** the message they send to the rest of the org is corrosive; learnings don't transfer.
- **Counterintuitive:** continuous deployment *is* a gentle-deployment technique when paired with customer-impact assessment.
