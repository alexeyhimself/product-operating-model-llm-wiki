# Cagan — Team Objectives: Summary

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Team Objectives – Summary" · svpg.com, 2020-03-04 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/team-objectives-summary/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The **ten keys** to effective team objectives, and the punchline: whether you use a formal technique like OKRs matters *less* than having a knowledgeable leader **sit with the team, explain the strategic context, name the problem, and define how success is measured** — then give the team the space to solve it. The consolidated reference for the [[okrs]] framework.

## What it backs
- frameworks: [[okrs]] (the ten keys; the "it's really just a conversation" framing)
- concepts: [[team-objectives]], [[product-strategy]]

## Notes for the coach
- **The essence, stripped of ceremony:** strategic context + a clear problem + a clear measure of success + space to solve. If a team lacks any of the four, that's the gap to coach.
- Objectives are normally refreshed **quarterly**; mid-quarter changes are the exception.
