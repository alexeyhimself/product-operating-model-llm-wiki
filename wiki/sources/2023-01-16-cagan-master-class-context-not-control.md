## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** A talk aimed at coaches / change-leaders — "leading complexity with context, not control" — with the sharpest "Team Red = agile theater" contrast.

- Direct challenge to the coaching community: "much of what you have been taught is not helping you — or your clients." Pairs with SVPG's coaching-supply thesis and [[model-as-product-coach]].
- "Context, not control" = the strategic-context framing of leadership; reinforces [[founder-style-leadership]] and [[product-leadership]].
