# Cagan — Product Manager vs. Product Owner Revisited

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Manager vs. Product Owner Revisited" · svpg.com, 2016-12-13 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-manager-vs-product-owner-revisited/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Cagan's update on his earlier position: **PM and PO must be one person**, AND the **PO role is a very minor part** of what a real PM does. CSPO training prepares someone for the Scrum-ritual aspects of the role — not for the full PM job. Many self-described "product owners" today have no clue what they're doing because they've conflated CSPO with PM training.
