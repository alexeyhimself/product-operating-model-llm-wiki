# Cagan — Top 10 Reasons for Weak Product

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Top 10 Reasons for Weak Product" · svpg.com, 2010-08-16 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/top-10-reasons-for-weak-product/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The canonical 10-item diagnosis: lack of vision, stakeholder-driven roadmap, missing test-and-learn culture, weak UX, little customer expertise, no discovery process, missing collaboration, focus on dates/features over results, lack of corporate courage, weak product leaders.
