# Cagan — Team Objectives: Commitments

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Team Objectives – Commitments" · svpg.com, 2020-03-04 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/team-objectives-commitments/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The **high-integrity commitment** within the OKR system: some things genuinely must ship by a date (trade show, contract, tax/holiday, ad campaign). The move — **do the discovery first** (often a **feasibility prototype**), validate against the [[the-four-big-risks|four risks]], *then* commit to a date leaders can count on. Commitments are **binary**, **tracked separately from key results**, often need **CTO sign-off**, and must be the **exception, not the rule** (or objectives degrade into a reformatted roadmap). A key companion to [[high-integrity-commitments]].

## Notes for the coach
- **Discovery-in-parallel-with-delivery is what makes a high-confidence date possible** — conventional Agile can't, because it commits before understanding.
- Platform-team dependency: a platform team can **inherit** the experience team's objective/KRs to deliver the needed API/service.
- Track commitments *independently* of KRs; raise the flag early at the first sign of trouble.
