# Cagan — Product Management as a Service Organization

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Management as a Service Organization" · svpg.com, 2010-01-13 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-management-as-a-service-organization/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Diagnosis of the **PM-as-service-org** anti-pattern: orgs that grew past startup but devolved into stakeholder-request-fulfillment, where the PM job is documentation, mediation, and dev-capacity allocation. Innovation grinds to a halt; talented people leave; the cycle institutionalizes. The setup article for [[2010-01-26-cagan-regaining-your-product-mojo|*Regaining Your Product Mojo*]] (its 10-step cure).

## Notes for the coach
- **"Roadmaps littered with hundreds of features; no real strategy."** Canonical tell.
- **The cure starts with customer expertise** — until the PM is the company-acknowledged expert on customers, no other intervention sticks.
- **The cultural failure is what makes this hard** — by the time the org gets here, the dysfunction is institutionalized; leadership commitment is required.
