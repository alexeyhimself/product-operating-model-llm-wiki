# Cagan — Assessing Customer Impact

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Assessing Customer Impact" · svpg.com, 2012-11-01 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/assessing-customer-impact/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The pre-release **three-bucket check** for continuous delivery: (1) not visible; (2) visible but harmless; (3) visible and potentially impactful. Bucket 3 requires coordination with product marketing and possibly delaying or dark-releasing.

## What it backs
- diagnostics: [[delivery-health]], [[discovery-health]]
- concepts: [[product-delivery]]
- principles: [[small-frequent-uncoupled-releases]]

## Notes for the coach
- **Bucket 3 is where coordination earns its keep.** Use as the operational complement to gentle deployment.
- **Product marketing as the coordination engine** — especially in enterprise contexts.
- The product manager flags the impact; the release manager / product marketing manages the communication.
