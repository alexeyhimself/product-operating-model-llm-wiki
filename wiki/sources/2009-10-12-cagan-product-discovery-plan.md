# Cagan — The Product Discovery Plan

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "The Product Discovery Plan" · svpg.com, 2009-10-12 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-discovery-plan/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Components of a discovery plan (core team, extended team, key stakeholders, customer development plan, key risks, user research/testing plan, vision/principles, documentation level). Carries the nine **weekly oversight questions** for the head of product to ask the discovery team.

## What it backs
- diagnostics: [[discovery-health]] (the nine oversight questions are the diagnostic's Socratic anchor)
- concepts: [[product-discovery]]

## Notes for the coach
- **The nine questions verbatim** are the cleanest single instrument to assess discovery rhythm.
- **"Feed the beast"** — Cagan's term for the failure mode where discovery becomes just-in-time spec writing for engineers.
- The "tools for a purpose, not a template" framing is important — don't drive discovery teams through a checklist.
