## Role
**Supporting** — a *working index* into the [[2023-01-01-toxboe-validation-patterns|Toxboe root source]]. ## What it is
- 60 rows — the *Validation Patterns* card-deck subset of Toxboe's 89-play collection.
- Columns: Validation domain · Name · Summary · Validates · Difficulty · Evidence strength · Synonyms · See also · Relevant metrics · How · Why.
- Sorted ascending by Evidence Strength — surfaces the "opinion is weak, real-behavior is strong" progression (NPS = 10 · Wizard of Oz = 90 · MMP = 95).

## What it backs
- **framework:** [[discovery-techniques-catalog]] — this sheet's ordering seeded the catalog's decision to sort within-risk descending by Evidence Strength, so a coach sees the strongest option per risk first.
