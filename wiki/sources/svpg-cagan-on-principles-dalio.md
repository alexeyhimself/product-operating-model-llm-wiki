# SVPG — "Principles" (Cagan on Ray Dalio)

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Principles" · svpg.com, 2017-12-27 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/principles/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Supporting.** A Cagan article on why he focuses on *principles* (techniques change; principles endure — the same reasoning behind this wiki) and an explicit **book recommendation**: Ray Dalio's *Principles: Life and Work*, which Cagan calls the most important business book of 2017 and encourages the teams he coaches to read.

## What it backs
- **Recommendation provenance** for [[principles-life-and-work]] (Dalio) in [[recommended-library]] — recommended by Cagan.
- Reinforces the wiki's own rationale: principles over techniques — relates to [[principles-over-process]] and the [[overview|first-principles approach]].

## Notes for the coach
- When recommending Dalio's *Principles*, cite this article as the Cagan endorsement.
- Cagan praises Dalio's work/life **principles** broadly but is explicit that Bridgewater's *radical-transparency culture* won't transplant to most tech companies — recommend the principles, not the culture wholesale.
