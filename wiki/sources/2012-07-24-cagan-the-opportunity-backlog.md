# Cagan — The Opportunity Backlog

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "The Opportunity Backlog" · svpg.com, 2012-07-24 (crediting Jeff Patton) — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/the-opportunity-backlog/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Reframes "the roadmap" as an **opportunity backlog** — a prioritized list of problems to explore in discovery, framed as opportunity assessments rather than features. The three opportunity-assessment questions (problem / persona / outcome of success) are here.

## What it backs
- diagnostics: [[discovery-health]], [[outcome-vs-output-orientation]]
- concepts: [[product-discovery]], [[team-objectives]]
- frameworks: would back a future `[[opportunity-assessment]]` page

## Notes for the coach
- **The opportunity backlog feeds discovery; the product backlog is the *output* of discovery and feeds delivery.** Important framing — many orgs collapse these.
- **Three questions:** what problem, for whom, how will we know if we succeed.
- Inputs to the opportunity backlog: product vision + product scorecard.
