# Cagan — Behind Every Great Product (MTP London 2016)

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · opening keynote, Mind the Product London 2016 · YouTube, published 2018-06-07 — [watch the original](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e0KJlYe2Rjk). Slim provenance card; the transcript is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The keynote revisiting Cagan's classic *Behind Every Great Product* essay (and Ben Horowitz's *Good Product Manager / Bad Product Manager*) — the clearest spoken statement of the true PM role and the "three ways teams run a product manager."

## Notes for the coach
- **"Three ways teams run a product manager":** (a) escalate every decision to their manager; (b) call a meeting every time a decision is needed (design-by-committee); (c) actually do the job — a real PM who solves hard problems. Only (c) is product management.
- Ben Horowitz's *Good Product Manager / Bad Product Manager* is the acknowledged ancestor; Cagan's *Behind Every Great Product* was his first blogging debut. The role is "even more important than it ever was."
