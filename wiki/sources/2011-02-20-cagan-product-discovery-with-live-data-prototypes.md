# Cagan — Product Discovery With Live-Data Prototypes

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Discovery With Live-Data Prototypes" · svpg.com, 2011-02-20 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-discovery-with-live-data-prototypes/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Distinguishes the **two primary fail-fast techniques**: user prototypes (disposable, qualitative, days) and live-data prototypes (real code, quantitative via A/B test, sometimes days). Different toolkits, different lessons; both used by the best companies. Live-data discovery A/B tests are *different* from product-optimization A/B tests — the variations are usually deep, not incremental.

## Notes for the coach
- **Bar for prototype quality is lower than for production** — feasibility/perf/localization can wait.
- **Two-week rule warning** — live-data prototypes can take longer; watch the timeline.
- **For established companies** — live-data prototypes reduce both risk and cost of innovation; usually the most-needed technique.
- Discovery A/B tests differ from optimization A/B tests in *scope of change* (deep variations vs. surface tweaks).
