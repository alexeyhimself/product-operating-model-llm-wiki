## Notes for the coach
- **The 10 misconceptions** span: idea vs product; implementing features vs solving problems; product owner vs true product manager; feature teams vs product teams; scaling with process vs scaling with leadership; and "one of the 10 is deliberately uncomfortable and gets to the product role."
