# Cagan — Coaching Tools: The One on One

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Coaching Tools — The One on One" · svpg.com, 2019-02-08 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/coaching-tools-the-one-on-one/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The canonical 10-point spec for the weekly PM 1:1 — purpose, relationship, onboarding, frequency, context-sharing, homework, thinking/acting like a PM, holistic view, feedback, continuous improvement. Plus six anti-patterns of bad managers.

## What it backs
- diagnostics: [[leadership-readiness]], [[pm-competency]]
- frameworks: [[coaching-the-pm]]
- competencies: [[product-coach]], [[product-leadership]]

## Notes for the coach
- **30+ minutes weekly, never canceled, primarily for the PM's development.** The sacred cadence.
- **Performance reviews should hold no surprises** — feedback is weekly.
- **The six anti-patterns** are useful when assessing whether a leader is actually coaching.
- **"The PM is our product"** — useful single line to reorient a manager from delivery-tracking to PM-development.
