# Toxboe — Idea Validation Playbook (Learning Loop, 2023)

> **Citation:** [[anders-toxboe|Anders Toxboe]] — *Idea Validation Playbook / Validation Patterns* — [[learning-loop|Learning Loop]], 2023 (continuously updated) — [https://learningloop.io/playbook-collections/validation-patterns/](https://learningloop.io/playbook-collections/validation-patterns/). Slim provenance card — the wiki links, it does not reproduce.

## Role
**Supporting reference — not primary canon.** This is the wiki's **first non-SVPG-canonical source**. The Toxboe catalog is used as a **broader menu of validation techniques a coach can pull from** alongside [[inspired|INSPIRED]] Part IV. Where Toxboe and Cagan diverge (framing, technique judgement), the wiki defers to Cagan and flags the divergence.

## What it is
- **89 product experiments** organised across four validation stages (problem · market · product · willingness-to-pay).
- Each play carries: name · summary · How · Why · Validates (Desirability/Feasibility/Viability) · Difficulty · **Evidence Strength** (numerical 5–95) · Relevant Metrics · See Also · Also Called · real-life examples with sourced citations.
- **60 of the 89** are also published as the *Validation Patterns* printed card deck — the subset that seeded [[2026-aleksei-validation-patterns-sheet|Aleksei's sheet]].

## Notes for the coach
- **Framing mismatch to flag.** Toxboe uses the design-thinking triangle **Desirability / Feasibility / Viability** — different from Cagan's **Value / Usability / Feasibility / Viability**. The wiki catalog remaps *desirability* → *value or usability* per play. When quoting Toxboe, keep his frame; when reasoning as coach, use Cagan's.
- **Cagan-critical techniques still listed.** NPS, PMF Survey, Focus Group, Closed-Ended Surveys, Sales Pitch (as validation) — the wiki tags these `use-with-care` with a one-line Cagan concern, but keeps them in the catalog so a coach can teach *why* they're weak evidence, not just avoid them silently.
- **Evidence Strength ≠ Cagan alignment.** Toxboe's score measures how strongly a technique's data reflects real intent. Cagan's alignment measures whether the technique addresses the right *risk*. Both matter. A high Toxboe score for a wrong-risk technique is still the wrong choice.
- **Individual play URLs.** Every play has its own page under `https://learningloop.io/plays/{slug}`, cited row-by-row in [[discovery-techniques-catalog]] so a coach can dive into any technique for full How/Why/tools/examples.
