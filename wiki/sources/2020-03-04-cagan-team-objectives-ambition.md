# Cagan — Team Objectives: Ambition

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Team Objectives – Ambition" · svpg.com, 2020-03-04 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/team-objectives-ambition/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Leaders must tell a team the **level of ambition** to strive for — **roof shot** (conservative, low-risk, high-likelihood; optimization fits) vs **moon shot** (very ambitious, ~10×, high-risk/high-reward) — optionally as a **confidence level** (e.g. ~80% vs ~20%). This is leaders **managing a portfolio of risk** (the poker analogy: don't force only \$1 or \$10,000 bets — allow the range), including running **multiple teams at the same problem** at different ambition levels. Ambition ≠ effort/urgency (culture), and ambition ≠ a high-integrity commitment (next in series).
