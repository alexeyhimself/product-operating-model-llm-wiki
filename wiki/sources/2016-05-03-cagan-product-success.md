# Cagan — Product Success

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Success" · svpg.com, 2016-05-03 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-success/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The sequel to [[2015-06-05-cagan-product-fail|*Product Fail*]] — the **10 keys to success** as the inversion of the 10 failure modes. Covers empowered teams, vision/strategy, outcome focus, competent PM, collaboration, discovery, four risks, MVP-as-test, delivery confidence, customer obsession.

## Notes for the coach
- **MVP-as-test, not MVP-as-product.** Source of the canonical Cagan position: "in every single case I have ever encountered, when the team spent the time and the money to build an actual QA'd product as their MVP, I have always been able to show them afterwards how they could have accomplished the same learning at much less cost and waste."
- **Three options for solving constraints:** CEO decides / design-by-committee / strong PM does the homework. Only the third produces strong product.
- The customer-obsession test ("how does the team respond to a production issue?") is a useful single-question diagnostic.
