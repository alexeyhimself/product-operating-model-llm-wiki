# Cagan — Your Business Plan is Wrong

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Your Business Plan is Wrong" · svpg.com, 2009-07-06 (with Josh Kopelman, First Round) — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/your-business-plan-is-wrong/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The canonical line "all business plans are wrong the moment they are saved" applied to product portfolio planning. Argues against treating detailed business cases as decision artifacts; embraces the pivot. The article that grounds Cagan's framing of pivots as discovery output, not failure.

- concepts: [[product-discovery]]
- diagnostics: [[discovery-health]], [[product-strategy-quality]]

## Notes for the coach
- **"Garbage in, garbage out"** — at very-early-stage, business-case numbers are fiction; the team, opportunity, and discovery process are the real signal.
- **PayPal/YouTube as pivot examples** — useful when pushing back on overinvestment in a specific feature theory.
- **Specs vs. discovery framing**: every additional word of spec makes you less open to new ideas — a sharp coaching line.
