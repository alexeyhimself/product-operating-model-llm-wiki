# Cagan — Getting Leadership Up to Speed (Product Thinking, Melissa Perri)

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan on *Product Thinking* with Melissa Perri · published 2024-02-14 — [watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eiYaad5zCxk). Slim provenance card; transcript **not reproduced**.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon) — a deep source for [[product-leadership]] and [[transformation]].** The management/leadership 80/20 split, the cost-center-vs-profit-center diagnosis, the ten keys to successful transformation (Cagan + [[lea-hickman|Hickman]]), and hiring-for-potential.

## What it backs
- competencies: [[product-leadership]] (Andy Grove: management = "don't know how," leadership = "don't want to"; first-level manager ~80% coaching, CPO the reverse; Bill Campbell "you cannot be a good manager without being a good coach"; "you'll be judged by your weakest PM")
- concepts: [[transformation]] (digital transformation → automation/cost-cutting; technology as **cost center vs profit center** — Ford vs Tesla; the CEO air-cover requirement; **The Guardian** 200-yr-old transformation, Judy Gibbons), diagnostics: [[transformation-readiness]] / [[model-maturity]] (the **ten keys to successful transformation**, #1 = CEO responsibility)
- frameworks: [[hiring-and-onboarding]] (hire on potential; first-job companies — Google/Amazon/Apple; apprenticeship)
- competencies: [[product-ops]] ("lipstick on a pig"; "rearranging deck chairs"; Stripe caveat)
