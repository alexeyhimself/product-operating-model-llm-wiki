# Cagan — Product Fail

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Fail" — *The Consequences of the Project Model* · svpg.com, 2015-06-05 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-fail/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The narrative version of the talk that lays out the **project model** — the way "the vast majority of companies still use to create products" — and the 10 specific dysfunctions it creates. Cagan later renamed "project model" explicitly to contrast it with "the product model."

## Notes for the coach
- **The 10 problems** are slightly different from [[2010-08-16-cagan-top-10-reasons-for-weak-product|Top 10 Reasons]] — these are about the *system*, not just the dimensions of weakness.
- **Source of the "time to money"** phrase used in current canon.
- **"All the risk is at the end"** — Waterfall's defining flaw.
- The narrative is useful for explaining to non-product audiences why their org keeps failing.
