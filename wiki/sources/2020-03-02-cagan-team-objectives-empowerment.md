# Cagan — Team Objectives: Empowerment

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Team Objectives – Empowerment" · svpg.com, 2020-03-02 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/team-objectives-empowerment/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** How team objectives *empower*: assign a **problem to solve** (not a feature) + the **strategic context** to understand the why. Defines the anatomy — **objective** (the qualitative problem) + **key results** (business-result measures, not activity/output), typically **two KRs** (a primary measure + a **quality "backstop"** so you don't win one metric by harming another). Crucially, **the actual KR values/targets come *from the team*** (ownership).

## What it backs
- frameworks: [[okrs]] (objective + KR structure; primary + backstop; KRs-from-team; qualitative objective examples)
- concepts: [[team-objectives]], [[empowered-with-problems-to-solve]], [[outcomes-over-output]]

## Notes for the coach
- **Second-most-common OKR failure:** listing activities/deliverables as key results (easy to ship a deliverable without solving the problem).
- **Backstop KR** — the quality guardrail (e.g. "reduce incorrect deliveries *while total deliveries keep growing*").
- Targets/timeframes come from the team, or ownership collapses; leaders provide the problem + strategic context.
