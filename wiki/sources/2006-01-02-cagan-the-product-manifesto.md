# Cagan — The Product Manifesto

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "The Product Manifesto" · svpg.com, 2006-01-02 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/the-product-manifesto/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** The canonical case for a written **product manifesto / principles** — a public declaration of beliefs that informs product trade-offs. Distinct from vision (where we're going) and roadmap (what we're building); principles are the *how-do-we-decide-when-it's-hard* layer.

## Notes for the coach
- **A manifesto bonds the team.** Useful for early-stage product teams looking for shared language.
- **Concise complement to vision and strategy** — Cagan: 8 core principles in the example referenced.
- The SVPG manifesto itself (linked in the article) is a useful exemplar.
