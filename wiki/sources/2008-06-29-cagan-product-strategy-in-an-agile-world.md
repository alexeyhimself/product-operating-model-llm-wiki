# Cagan — Product Strategy in an Agile World

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Product Strategy in an Agile World" · svpg.com, 2008-06-29 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/product-strategy-in-an-agile-world/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** A 2008 piece responding to the misconception that "agile means no long-term direction." Establishes that product strategy and Agile are compatible; agile changes the roadmap, not the strategy. Distinguishes business strategy from product strategy.

## What it backs
- diagnostics: [[product-strategy-quality]]
- concepts: [[product-strategy]]

## Notes for the coach
- Useful for an old, recurring objection: "we're agile so we don't do strategy."
- **Business strategy ≠ product strategy.** A common conflation.
- Strategy is "the bridge between business strategy and product roadmap."
