# Cagan — Product Discovery, Strategy & Empowered Teams (Product Faculty AMA)

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · AMA, Toronto Product Mastery Series (Product Faculty) · YouTube, 2020-04-23 — [watch the original](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=saF8fViOrO8). Slim provenance card; the transcript is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** An AMA across discovery, strategy, and empowered teams — notable for the clearest statement that coaching (not talent selection) is what the best companies do.

## What it backs
- competencies: [[product-coaching]] ("the reason Google/Amazon/Apple/Netflix people do good work is their managers coach them — it's not who they hire")
- concepts: [[empowered-product-teams]], [[product-discovery]] (remote differs for discovery vs delivery), [[team-topology]]

## Notes for the coach
- **"Not who they hire — they coach you."** The recurring Cagan rebuttal to "we can't get Google-caliber people": the difference is coaching, not the talent filter. Strong provenance for [[product-coaching]] and the model-as-coach lineage.
- Remote work: the nature of remote employees differs for **discovery** (harder) vs **delivery** (more tolerable). Reinforces the colocation discussion.
