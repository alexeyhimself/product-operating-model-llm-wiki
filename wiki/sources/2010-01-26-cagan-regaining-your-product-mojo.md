# Cagan — Regaining Your Product Mojo

> **Citation:** Marty Cagan · "Regaining Your Product Mojo" · svpg.com, 2010-01-26 — [read the original](https://www.svpg.com/regaining-your-product-mojo/). Slim provenance card; the article is **not reproduced** here.

## Role
**Primary (SVPG canon).** Ten concrete moves for a product org that has been demoted to a service organization: customer expertise, rapid-response team, embrace UX, design in customer acquisition, collaborate with developers, mitigate risks, think big, data not opinions, involve executives, evangelize. The companion to [[2010-08-16-cagan-top-10-reasons-for-weak-product|*Top 10 Reasons for Weak Product*]] — what to do *about* it.

## What it backs
- diagnostics: [[stakeholder-relationship-health]], [[discovery-health]], [[product-strategy-quality]]
- competencies: [[product-manager]], [[product-leadership]]

## Notes for the coach
- **The 10 moves are practical and immediate** — useful when a PM is trapped in a feature-factory and wants individual moves they can make today.
- **"Data not opinions"** is the single biggest unlock for stakeholder relationships.
- **Rapid-response team** is a useful concept for shielding the discovery team from urgent-but-not-strategic interruptions.
