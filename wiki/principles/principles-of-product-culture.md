# Principles of Product Culture

> The fifth and final group of [product model first principles](overview.md). Culture is what makes the other four groups stick: it's the difference between a team that *has* empowerment on paper and one that *lives* it.

These four principles define the culture strong product companies build: lead with **principles, not process**; **trust over control**; **innovation over predictability**; and **learning over failure**.

17. **[[principles-over-process|Principles over Process]]** — scale with leaders and judgment, not process that prevents judgment.
18. **[[trust-over-control|Trust over Control]]** — servant leadership and active coaching; lead with context, not control.
19. **[[innovation-over-predictability|Innovation over Predictability]]** — "100% predictability = 0% innovation"; high-integrity commitments are the exception.
20. **[[learning-over-failure|Learning over Failure]]** — an experiment has no success/failure, only "what did we learn?"
